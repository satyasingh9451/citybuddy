Got Questions?

Everything you need to know before booking your first City Buddy.

Is City Buddy safe for women? +
Absolutely. All buddies are ID-verified before onboarding. Every meetup is in a public location only. We also offer female-only buddy matching on request. Your safety is our highest priority.
How do I pay for a session? +
Payment is confirmed after we match you with a buddy. We accept UPI, GPay, PhonePe, and bank transfer. You only pay after your booking is confirmed — no advance charges.
Can I choose a specific buddy? +
You can mention preferences in your booking form (age range, interests, communication style). We do our best to match accordingly, though specific buddy requests cannot be guaranteed.
What if I feel uncomfortable during a meetup? +
You can end any session at any time, no questions asked. Our support line is active during all meetups. Simply text or call us and we'll handle the rest. Your comfort always comes first.
Is this a dating or romantic service? +
No. City Buddy is strictly a social companionship service. Any romantic, sexual, or inappropriate behaviour leads to immediate and permanent removal from the platform. Zero tolerance — always.
How quickly is my booking confirmed? +
We review all bookings within 4 hours and send a match confirmation to your email. For same-day bookings, please contact us on WhatsApp directly for faster processing.
